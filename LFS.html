Welcome to Latjor Sport Foundation

Welcome to Latjor Sport Foundation! We are dedicated to empowering young people through sports and talent development.

Our Mission

To provide opportunities and sports equipment for young people, especially those in refugee camps, to participate in sports and develop their talents. Together, we inspire, nurture, and build a brighter future through the power of sports.

Our Vision

A just and inclusive society that values peace and unity, where participation in sports and talent development is nurtured.

Our Goal

To identify and engage refugee children in community sports competitions for possible talent pathways and pipelines.
